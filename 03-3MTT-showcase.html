Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Can I use two sittings for JAMB admission?

A: Yes, most universities accept WAEC/NECO combined results (max: 2 sittings)

Q: Can I change my institution after writing UTME?

A: Yes, through JAMB Change of Institution Form (available online)

Q: What is the age limit for JAMB?

A: Candidates must be at least 16 years old at the time of admission
