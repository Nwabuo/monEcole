What's About JAMB...?

The Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board, (JAMB), is the Nigerian agency responsible for conducting the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME), ‚Äî the standardized exam used to determine candidates' eligibility for admission into Nigerian universities, polytechnics, colleges of education, and other tertiary institutions.

What is JAMB UTME ?

UTME is a standardized entrance examination conducted by JAMB for prospective undergraduates seeking admission into Nigerian universities, polytechnics, colleges of education, and other tertiary institutions.

The UTME is a 2 hour multiple-choice test in four subjects, with a compulsory paper, the use of English, constituting Part 1. Part II of the examinatioon consists of test in three subject of a candidates choice. Prospective Candidates are adviced to seek the advice of their teachers, parents/Guardian and guidance in or career councelor in their choice of elective subjects for the UTME.

ADMISSION REQUIREMENT

Prospective Candidates for the UTME are to note that for them to secure admissioninto tertiarty educatrion program, they have to:

  1. - attain an appreciable standard of pass in the UTME in the relevant subjects.
  2. - have credit passes in five relevant subjects in the appropriate WAEC/ NECO/Teachers's Grade examination.
